Social Intelligence

Orcas live in tight family groups (pods), and many stay with their mothers for life.

Unique Dialects

Different pods have distinct vocal patterns, almost like cultural languages passed down generations.

Global Presence

Orcas are found in every ocean, from polar seas to tropical waters.

Hunting Strategy

They often hunt cooperatively, using learned techniques like wave-washing seals off ice.

Top Predator

As apex predators, wild adult orcas have virtually no natural predators.

Size & Power

Males can exceed 8 meters (26 ft) and have dorsal fins up to 1.8 meters (6 ft) tall.

Life Journey (Typical)

  • 0-1 yrCalves stay close to mom and learn movement, social calls, and pod behavior.
  • 2-10Young orcas develop hunting awareness and social roles through observation and play.
  • 10-30Adults become skilled hunters and key members in pod cooperation and leadership.
  • 30+Older females can guide pods with memory of migration routes and food locations.
